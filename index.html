Hi, I'm Avishek Bhattacharjee!

I am a Research Scientist at MTX Group Inc. working in the domain of Computer Vision and Deep Learning.

Before joining MTX Group, I spent three wonderful years as an MS (by Research) scholar at IIT Madras, where I researched in the broad area of Face Recognition (FR) with primary focus on Pose-Invariant FR, Makeup Invariant FR, and FR in Surveillance and Classroom scenarios.
